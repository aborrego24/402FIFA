Karsin

Karsin is a highly skilled and aggressive player who transferred from Wash U St. Louis League. He is known for being one of the league's top individual performers, boasting multiple double-digit win streaks against fellow players. His aggressive playing style sets him apart and makes him a formidable opponent on the pitch.

1st annual World Cup

Karsin came into the tournament on a 10 game win streak. That includes 2 games against Alejandro.
