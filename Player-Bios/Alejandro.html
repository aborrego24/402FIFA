Alejandro

Alejandro is not just a formidable FIFA player, but also the mastermind behind the league's website, overseeing its management with dedication and passion as the League Commissioner. On the pitch, Alejandro's exceptional skills have won him the Annual World Cup, while his jaw-dropping goals have secured him the Goal of the Week award an impressive three times, solidifying his status as a true FIFA superstar.
